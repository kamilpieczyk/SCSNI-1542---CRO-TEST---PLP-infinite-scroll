Description

The Maisy sofa collection would make a stylish addition to any home, combining classic designs with plain weave fabric, which is good/durable for day-to-day living and micro chenille fabric which is soft and tactile velvet, with a quality luxury feel to it.

With a choice of fabric colour options, each piece is complete with foam seats as standard, with serpentine seat springs ensuring adding support and comfort. Available in the collection are thee sizes of sofas and three stylish chairs with coordinating footstools to complement your new sofa.

Make the Maisy sofa collection even more affordable and take advantage of our 4 years interest-free credit offer, 0% APR representative (minimum spend 320).
